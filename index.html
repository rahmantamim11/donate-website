Food for a Family

9TYs came up with a plan to relieve the pains of hunger. Food arrangements are made from self-given and donations received from people. After collecting the food, the volunteers of 9TYs deliver the food to the helpless, hungry, and needy people.

We see many children around us who have no homes, roam the streets, have no education and do criminal activities. We will teach those children to read and write. We will give them books, notebooks, pens, bags, etc for free in this program.
